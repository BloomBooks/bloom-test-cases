17. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the Video preview appears on the right side of the page.<br>
18. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the 'Record' button.<br>
19. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that it starts video recording.<br>
20. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the Stop button.<br>
