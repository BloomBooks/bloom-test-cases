# Steps

## Step

### Action

```html
<p>Setup Steps:<br>
1. Launch Bloom application with the English book collection.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Action Steps:<br>
1. Verify that the &lsquo;Sources for New Books&rsquo; section appeared on the left pane.<br>
2. Click the &lsquo;Moon and the Cap' from the Sample Shells in the &lsquo;Source for New Books&rsquo; section.<br>
3. Verify that the &lsquo;Moon and the Cap' book preview is opened.<br>
4. Click the &lsquo;MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE&rsquo; button.<br>
5. Verify that the &lsquo;Moon and the Cap book&rsquo; is opened in the Edit mode.<br>
6. Click on the Page "3" thumbnail on the Pages pane.<br>
7. Verify that the Page "3" is opened in the edit mode.<br>
8. Verify that the texts are aligned to the top in the text box.<br>
9. Turn on the Change Layout Toggle button.<br>
10 Verify the Change Layout Toggle button becomes turned ON.<br>
11. Verify that the texts are overlaid by the Text Box gear button.<br>
12. Click the gear button from the Text box.&nbsp;<br>
13. Verify the Text box Properties dialog appears.<br>
14. Click on the Alignment tab from the Text box Properties dialog.<br>
15. Verify the 3 numbers of Vertical Alignment buttons appear.<br>
16. Verify the first button is selected by default.<br>
17. Click on the Second Vertical Alignment button.<br>
18. Verify the Second button gets selected.<br>
19. Click on the outside of the Text box Properties dialog.<br>
20. Verify the Text box Properties dialog closes.<br>
21. Turn off the Change Layout Toggle button.<br>
22. Verify the Change Layout Toggle button turned OFF.<br>
23. Verify that the texts are aligned to the middle in the text box.<br>
24. Turn on the Change Layout Toggle button.<br>
25. Verify the Change Layout Toggle button turned ON.<br>
26. Verify that the text is overlaid by the Text Box gear button.<br>
27. Click the gear button from the Text box.&nbsp;<br>
28. Verify the Text box Properties dialog appears.<br>
29. Click on the Alignment tab from the Text box Properties dialog.<br>
30. Verify the 3 numbers of Vertical Alignment buttons appear.<br>
31. Verify the second button is selected.<br>
32. Click on the Third Vertical Alignment button.<br>
33. Verify the Third Vertical Alignment button gets selected.<br>
34. Click on the outside of the Text box Properties dialog.<br>
35. Verify the Text box Properties dialog closes.<br>
36. Turn off the Change Layout Toggle button.<br>
37. Verify the Change Layout Toggle button turned OFF.<br>
38. Verify that the texts are aligned to the bottom in the text box.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Cleanup Steps:<br>
1. Delete the &ldquo;Moon and the Cap&rdquo; book.<br>
2. Close the Bloom application.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
```
