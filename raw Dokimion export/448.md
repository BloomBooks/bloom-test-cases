<p>Setup Steps:<br>

3. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the book opens in the Edit mode.&nbsp;<br>
4. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the Add Page button.<br>
5. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the 'Just text' template from the Basic Pages heading.<br>
6. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the Add Page button.<br>
7. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify 'Just text' page is added<br>
8. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click on the text box.<br>
9. &nbsp; &nbsp;Type &ldquo;A hungry fox once looked everywhere for food.&rdquo; in the text box.<br>
10. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the word count shows &lsquo;8&rsquo; on the right most pane.&nbsp;<br>
11. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the text box.&nbsp;<br>
12. &nbsp; &nbsp;Press Ctrl+V to paste the text.&nbsp;<br>
13. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the word count increases. (eg., it shows &lsquo;13) on the right most pane.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Cleanup Steps:<br>
1. &nbsp; &nbsp;Close the Notepad editor.<br>
2. &nbsp; &nbsp;Delete the Leveled Reader book.&nbsp;<br>
