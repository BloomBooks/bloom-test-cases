# Steps

## Step

### Action

```html
<p>Setups Steps:<br>
1. Launch Bloom application.<br>
&nbsp;<br>
Action Steps:<br>
1. Click "The Moon and the Cap" book from the Sample Shells under Sources For New Books.<br>
2. Verify the "The Moon and the Cap" book preview displays.<br>
3. Verify the MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button appears.<br>
4. Click on the MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button.<br>
5. Verify the "The Moon and the Cap" book appears in the Edit Mode.<br>
6. Verify the "Choose a page size and orientation" dropdown displays "A5 Portrait" by default.<br>
7. Click the "Choose a page size and orientation" dropdown.<br>
8. Verify that "Legal Landscape" is listed as an option.<br>
9. Click the &ldquo;Legal Landscape&rdquo; Option.<br>
10. Verify the page size and orientation turns from "A5 Portrait" to &ldquo;Legal Landscape&rdquo;.<br>
11. Click on the Publish tab.<br>
12. Verify the PDF &amp; Print button appears.<br>
13. Click the PDF &amp; Print button.<br>
14. Verify the Publish to PDF &amp; Print page appears.<br>
15. Verify the Booklet Mode displays the following buttons : Simple, Booklet Cover, Booklet Insides.<br>
16. Click the "Simple" button.<br>
17. Verify the Progress dialog appears.<br>
18. Verify the Creating PDF.. message displays in the Progress dialog.&nbsp;<br>
19. Verify the Progress dialog closes.<br>
20. Verify that the PDF file is generated and displayed.<br>
21. Verify that the PDF file displayed as Legal Landscape orientation.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Cleanup Steps:<br>
1. Delete &lsquo;The Moon and the Cap&rsquo; book.<br>
2. Close Bloom application.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
```
