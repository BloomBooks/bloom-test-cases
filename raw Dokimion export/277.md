# Steps

## Step

### Action

```html
<p>Setup Steps:<br>
1. Install Annapurna SIL font from &ldquo;https://software.sil.org/annapurna/download/&rdquo;<br>
2. Launch Bloom application.</p>
<p><br>
Action Steps:<br>
1. Click The "Moon and the Cap" book from the "Sample Shells" pane.<br>
2. Verify that the "Moon and the Cap" book is displayed in the "Right pane".<br>
3. Click the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.<br>
4. Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.<br>
5. Click " Page 2" from the "Pages" pane.<br>
6. Verify that the "Page 2" is displayed in the "Right pane".<br>
7. Click on the text box from page 2.<br>
8. Click the "Gear" icon which appears at the bottom left corner of the Page in "Right Pane".<br>
9. Verify that the "Format" dialog box is displayed..<br>
10. Click the "Characters" tab.<br>
11. Click the Font "Dropdown" menu.<br>
12. Verify the newly installed "Annapurna" font appears in the menu.<br>
10. Click the "Annapurna SIL " font.<br>
11. Verify that the text font is changed to "Annapurna SIL" font.<br>
&nbsp;<br>
Cleanup Steps:<br>
1. Remove Annapurna SIL font.<br>
2. Close Bloom application.</p>
```
