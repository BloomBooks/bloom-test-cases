### Action

```html
<p>Setup Steps:<br>
1. &nbsp; &nbsp;Launch Bloom application<br>
2. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the Settings button.<br>
3. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the Settings dialog opens.&nbsp;<br>
4. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the Bloom Subscription tab.<br>
5. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the Bloom Subscription tab opens.&nbsp;<br>
6. &nbsp; &nbsp;Enter 'Test-727011-1339' in the Subscription code.&nbsp;<br>
7. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the Restart button appears instead of the OK button.<br>
8. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the Restart button.<br>
9. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify Bloom gets restarted.<br>
10. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the Other Collection button.<br>
11. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify Open/Create Collections dialog opens.&nbsp;<br>
12. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click any Team Collection book. (eg., Tamil Share Books)<br>
13. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the Team Collection book opens.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Action Steps:<br>
1. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click a Team Collection book which is checked out to you.<br>
2. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click a Basic book.<br>
3. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button is available on the right pane.<br>
4. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button.<br>
5. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the Book opens in the edit view.<br>
6. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the Front Cover page thumbnail.<br>
7. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the Front cover page opens.&nbsp;<br>
8. &nbsp; &nbsp;Enter &lsquo;Shared Book&rsquo; in the title box.<br>
9. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the Collections tab.<br>
10. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the book appears under the Book collection.&nbsp;<br>
11. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify your Avatar appears on the book thumbnail.&nbsp;<br>
12. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify a triangle button appears at the bottom right corner of the book thumbnail.<br>
13. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click on the triangle button.&nbsp;<br>
14. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify a popup menu opens.&nbsp;<br>
15. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click More option.<br>
16. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify Export to Spreadsheet option is available.&nbsp;<br>
17. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify Import Content from Spreadsheet is available.<br>
18. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click outside of the popup menu.<br>
19. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the popup menu closes.<br>
20. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click a Team Collection book which is checked out to other users.(without your Avatar)<br>
21. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the other user Avatar appears on the book thumbnail.<br>
22. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the other user's Avatar book thumbnail.<br>
23. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify a triangle button appears at the bottom right corner of the (other user) book thumbnail.<br>
24. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click on the triangle button.&nbsp;<br>
25. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify a popup menu opens.&nbsp;<br>
26. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click More option.<br>
27. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify Export to Spreadsheet option is enabled.&nbsp;<br>
28. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify Import Content from Spreadsheet is disabled.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Cleanup Steps:<br>
1. &nbsp; &nbsp;Delete the Team Collection book.<br>
2. &nbsp; &nbsp;Delete the Subscription Code.<br>
3. &nbsp; &nbsp;Close Bloom application.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
```
