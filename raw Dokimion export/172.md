```html
<p>Setup:<br>
1. &nbsp; &nbsp;Launch Bloom application.<br>
&nbsp;<br>
Action Steps:<br>
1. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the &ldquo;Basic Book&rdquo; under the &ldquo;Templates&rdquo; area.<br>
2. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the &nbsp;&ldquo;Basic Book&rdquo; template is displayed in the "Right pane".<br>
3. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.<br>
4. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.<br>
5. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the "Front Cover" page from the "Pages" section selected by default<br>
6. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the &ldquo;Front Cover&rdquo; page layout is displayed on the right side<br>
7. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click on the "Book Title" textbox in the "Front Cover" page<br>
8. &nbsp; &nbsp;Type the title as "Test"<br>
9. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click on the "Add Page" button from the "Pages" section<br>
10. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the "Add Page..." dialog opens<br>
11. &nbsp; &nbsp;Select the "Basic Text &amp; Picture" template from the "Basic Pages"<br>
12. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the "ADD PAGE" button<br>
13. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that Page 1 is added under the Pages section on the left side<br>
14. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the layout of Page 1 is displayed on the right side<br>
15. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the Hamburger menu from the top right corner<br>
16. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the "Talking Book Tool" appears<br>
17. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click inside the "Textbox" in the Page "1" layout<br>
18. &nbsp; &nbsp;Type the sentence "Text Box 1: Test Talking Book Tool"<br>
19. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the the sentence "Text Box 1: Test Talking Book Tool" is highlighted<br>
20. &nbsp; &nbsp;Switch on the "Change Layout" toggle button above page "1"<br>
21. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the "Text Box" pane appeared on the page "1"&nbsp;<br>
22. &nbsp; &nbsp;Hover the mouse over the Text Box pane<br>
23. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the + buttons appears on the Text box<br>
24. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click on the + button<br>
25. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the &ldquo;Picture, Video, or Text&rdquo; options appear<br>
26. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click on the "Text" Option<br>
27. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the new "Text Box" added on the Page 1 layout<br>
28. &nbsp; &nbsp;Switch off the "Change Layout" toggle button above page "1"<br>
29. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click on the newly added Textbox in the Page 1 layout<br>
30. &nbsp; &nbsp;Type the sentence "Text Box 2: Test Talking Book Tool"<br>
31. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the the sentence "Text Box 2: Test Talking Book Tool" is highlighted<br>
&nbsp;<br>
Cleanup Steps:<br>
1. &nbsp; &nbsp;Delete the Basic book.<br>
2. &nbsp; &nbsp;Close the Bloom application.</p>
```
