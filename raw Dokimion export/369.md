<p>Setup Steps:<br>

4. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.<br>
5. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the "Front cover" page.<br>

7. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the &ldquo;Credits Page&rdquo;.&nbsp;<br>
8. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the credits page preview is displayed in the right pane.<br>

23. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click &ldquo;EDIT THIS BOOK&rdquo;.<br>
24. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.<br>
25. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the &ldquo;Credits Page&rdquo;&nbsp;<br>
