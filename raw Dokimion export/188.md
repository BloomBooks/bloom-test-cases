Action Steps:</p>

3. Click the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.<br>
4. Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.<br>
5. Click "Page 5" from the "Pages" pane.<br>
6. Verify that the "Page 5" is displayed in the "Right pane".<br>
7. Click on the text box in the "Right pane".<br>
8. Click the "Gear" icon which appears at the bottom left corner of the Page in "Right Pane".<br>
9. Verify that the "Format" dialog box is displayed..<br>
10. Click the "Characters" tab.<br>
11. Click the color pallet in the "Characters" tab..<br>
12. Verify &ldquo;Text Color&rdquo; dialog box is displayed.<br>
13. Select the &ldquo;Red&rdquo; color in the &ldquo;Text Color&rdquo; dialog box.<br>
14. Verify that the text color in the "Page 5" is in red color.<br>
14. Click the &ldquo;OK&rdquo; button in the &ldquo;Text Color&rdquo; dialog box.<br>
15. Verify &ldquo;Text Color&rdquo; dialog box is closed.<br>
17. Click on the text box in the "Right pane".<br>
18. Verify that the "Format" dialog box is closed.<br>
16. Verify that the text color in the "Page 5" is in red color.<br>
17. Click the "Gear" icon which appears at the bottom left corner of the Page in "Right Pane".<br>
18. Verify that the "Format" dialog box is displayed..<br>
19. Click the "Characters" tab.<br>
20. Click the &ldquo;Bold&rdquo; option under the &ldquo;Emphasis&rdquo; area.<br>
21. Click on the text box in the "Right pane".<br>
22. Verify that the "Format" dialog box is closed.<br>
23. Verify that the texts are "Bold" in the text area.<br>
24. Click the "Gear" icon which appears at the bottom left corner of the Page in "Right Pane".<br>
25. Verify that the "Format" dialog box is displayed..<br>
26. Click the "Characters" tab.<br>
27. Click the &ldquo;Italic&rdquo; option under the &ldquo;Emphasis&rdquo; area.<br>
28. Click on the text box in the "Right pane".<br>
29. Verify that the "Format" dialog box is closed.<br>
30. Verify that the texts are now both "Bold &amp; Italic" in the text area.<br>
31. Click the "Gear" icon which appears at the bottom left corner of the Page in "Right Pane".<br>
32. Verify that the "Format" dialog box is displayed..<br>
33. Click the "Characters" tab.<br>
34. Click the &ldquo;Underline&rdquo; option under the &ldquo;Emphasis&rdquo; area.<br>
35. Click on the text box in the "Right pane".<br>
36. Verify that the "Format" dialog box is closed.<br>
37. Verify that the texts are now "Bold, Italic &amp; Underlined" in the text area.</p>
