# Steps

## Step

### Action

```html
<p>Setup Steps:<br>
1. &nbsp; &nbsp;Open Google Word document.<br>
2. &nbsp; &nbsp;Enter the long text &nbsp;&lsquo;The Greek king Midas did a good deed for a Satyr. This prompted Dionysus, the god of wine, to grant him a wish. Midas asked for everything he touched to turn to gold. Dionysus&rsquo; warned him not to do so, but Midas could not be swayed. Midas excitedly started touching everything and turning them into gold. Soon, he became hungry. But he couldn&rsquo;t eat anything because even his food turned to gold.&rsquo;<br>
3. &nbsp; &nbsp;Press Ctrl+Shift+C<br>
4. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the Word Count dialogue box opens.&nbsp;<br>
5. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify it shows &lsquo;72&rsquo; in the Word Count field.&nbsp;<br>
6. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the Cancel button.<br>
7. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the Word Count dialogue box closes.&nbsp;<br>
8. &nbsp; &nbsp;Press Ctrl+A to select all.<br>
9. &nbsp; &nbsp;Press Ctrl+C to copy the long text.<br>
10. &nbsp; &nbsp;Launch Bloom application.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Action Steps:<br>
1. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the 'Leveled Reader' template under Specialized Templates heading. (which appears at the lower left pane in Collectins view)<br>
2. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button.<br>
3. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the book opens in the Edit mode.&nbsp;<br>
4. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the Add Page button.<br>
5. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the 'Just text' template from the Basic Pages heading.<br>
6. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the Add Page button.<br>
7. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify 'Just text' page is added<br>
8. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click on the text box.<br>
9. &nbsp; &nbsp;Press Ctrl+V to paste the long text.&nbsp;<br>
10. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the Word count on the right most pane.&nbsp;<br>
11. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify the Word count shows &lsquo;72&rsquo;&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Cleanup Steps:<br>
1. &nbsp; &nbsp;Close Google document.<br>
2. &nbsp; &nbsp;Delete Leveled Reader book.<br>
3. &nbsp; &nbsp;Close Bloom application.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
```
