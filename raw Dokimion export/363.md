```html
<p>Setup Steps:<br>
1. Launch Bloom application.&nbsp;<br>
&nbsp;<br>
Action Steps:<br>
1. Click on the "Basic Book" Templates from the Sources For New Books.<br>
2. Verify the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button appears on the Right side pane.<br>
3. Click on the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.<br>
4. Verify the Basic Book opens in the Edit Mode.<br>
5. Click on the Front Cover page from the Left pane.<br>
6. Verify the Front Cover page displays to Edit.<br>
7. Click on the Title Box from the Front Cover page<br>
8. Type Title as Test.<br>
9. Click on the "Add Page +" button.<br>
10. Verify the "Add Page..." dialog appears.<br>
11. Select the Basic Text &amp; Picture Template.<br>

14. Verify the Top down triangle button appears at the bottom-right corner of the Page "1" &nbsp;thumbnail.<br>
15. Click on the Collections tab.<br>
16. Verify the Settings button appears in the Standard Toolbar.<br>
17. Click on the Settings button.<br>
18. Verify the Settings dialog appears.<br>
19. Click on the Book Making tab.<br>
20. Verify the Special Script Settings link button appears under the Default Font for English drop down.<br>
21. Click the Special Script Settings link button.<br>
22. Verify Special Script Settings dialog opens.&nbsp;<br>
23. Verify that the checkbox "This script is written right to left like Arabic, Hebrew, and Urdu." is unchecked by default.<br>

25. Verify that the checkbox becomes checked after selection.<br>
26. Click the OK button.<br>
27. Verify Special Script Settings dialog closes.&nbsp;<br>
28. Click the Restart button from the Settings dialog box.<br>
29. Verify Bloom gets restarted.<br>
30. Click the EDIT THIS BOOK button on the Right side pane.<br>
31. Verify the Page "1" thumbnail appears on the left side.<br>
32. Verify the Top down triangle button appears at the bottom-right corner of the Page "1" &nbsp;thumbnail.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
```
