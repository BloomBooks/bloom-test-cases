# Steps

## Step

### Action

```html
<p>Setup Steps:</p>
<p>1. &nbsp; &nbsp;Open the web browser<br>
2. &nbsp; &nbsp;Navigate to the URL &ldquo;https://bloomlibrary.org/page/resources/all-bloom-installers&rdquo;.<br>
3. &nbsp; &nbsp;Download any of the older versions of the &ldquo;Bloom&rdquo; and install it.<br>
4. &nbsp; &nbsp; Launch Bloom application.<br>
&nbsp;<br>
Action Steps:</p>
<p>1. &nbsp; Click the &ldquo;Get Help(?)&rdquo; icon from the standard toolbar.<br>
2. &nbsp; Verify that the &ldquo;Help&rdquo; menu is displayed.<br>
3. &nbsp; Click the &ldquo;Check For New Version&rdquo; from the displayed menu.<br>
4. &nbsp; Verify that the menu is closed.<br>
5. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the &rdquo;A new version of Bloom is available&rdquo; popup is displayed in the right bottom corner of the application.<br>
6. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the &ldquo;Update Now&rdquo; hyper link from the displayed popup.<br>
7. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the &ldquo;Downloading update to (Current version) &rdquo; popup is displayed<br>
8. &nbsp; &nbsp;After downloading, verify that the &ldquo;Applying Updates&rdquo; popup is displayed on the right bottom corner of the application.<br>
9. &nbsp; &nbsp;After updating the application, verify that the &ldquo;Update for (Current version) is ready&rdquo; popup is displayed on the right bottom corner of the application.<br>
10. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the &nbsp;&ldquo;Restart Bloom to Update&rdquo; Hyperlink in the &nbsp;&ldquo;Update for (Current version) is ready&rdquo; popup.<br>
11. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the &ldquo;Bloom&rdquo; is restarted.<br>
12. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the &ldquo;Get Help(?)&rdquo; icon from the standard toolbar.<br>
13. &nbsp; &nbsp;Click the &ldquo;Check For New Version&rdquo; from the displayed menu.<br>
14. &nbsp; &nbsp;Verify that the &ldquo;Your Bloom is up to date&rdquo; popup is displayed on the right bottom corner of the application.&nbsp;<br>
&nbsp;<br>
Cleanup Steps:</p>
<p>1. &nbsp; &nbsp;Close Bloom application.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
```
