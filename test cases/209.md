# Steps

**Setups Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Verify that the ‘Sources for New Books’ section appeared on the left pane
2. Click on the ‘Basic book’ from the Templates
3. Verify that the template preview is opened
4. Click on the ‘MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE’ button
5. Verify that the template is opened in the edit mode.
6. Click on the ‘Add page’ button
7. Verify that the ‘Add Page…’ dialog opened
8. Click on the ‘Just Text’  template
9. Click on the ‘Add Page’ button
10. Verify that the ‘Just Text’ template is added to the book
11. Click on the text box.
12. Paste the text “word joiner: x⁠x”
13. Press ‘Ctrl + Shift + Space’ keys from the keyboard
14. Verify that the ‘Zero width Space’ and ‘Word Joiner’ invisibles tooltip appeared
15. Paste the text “en quad: x x”
16. Press ‘Ctrl + Shift + Space’ keys from the keyboard
17. Verify that the ‘En Quad’ tooltip appeared

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close Bloom application.
