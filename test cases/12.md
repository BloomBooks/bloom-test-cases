# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Disconnect the Internet Cable from the Computer.
2. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the Get Help(?) button from the bloom toolbar.
2. Verify an option menu opens.
3. Click Report a problem option.
4. Verify Report a problem dialog opens.
5. Enter 'Testing' under 'What were you doing?' option.
6. Click the SUBMIT button.
7. Verify a message says "Bloom was not able to submit your report directly to our server. Please retry or email C:\Users\UserName\AppData\Local\Temp\Report 2025-10-27 14.10.34Z.zip to issues@bloomlibrary.org." appear in the dialog.
8. Click the Close button.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Connect the Internet Cable to the Computer.
2. Close Bloom application.
