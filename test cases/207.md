# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Verify that the ‘Sources for New Books’ section appeared on the left pane
2. Click on the ‘Moon and the Cap’ book from the Templates in the ‘Sample Shells’ section
3. Verify that the template preview is opened
4. Click on the ‘MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE’ button
5. Verify that the template is opened in the edit mode.
6. Click on the page ‘1’ thumbnail from the ‘Pages’ pane.
7. Verify that the user redirected to the 1st Page
8. Verify that 'All of us went to the fun fair.' text appeared
9. Click on the text box.
10. Press ‘Ctrl + A’ from the keyboard
11. Verify that all the text contents are selected
12. Press ‘Ctrl + X’ from the keyboard
13. Verify that the text content is cut and removed from the text box
14. Press ‘Ctrl + Z’ from the keyboard
15. Verify that the removed text is restored
16. Double click on the word ‘fair’
17. Press ‘Ctrl + U’ from the keyboard
18. Verify that the word ‘fair’ appeared as the underlined text
19. Press ‘Ctrl + Z’ from the keyboard
20. Verify that the underlined text style is removed for the word ‘fair’.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the book ‘Moon and the Cap’ from the collections
2. Close Bloom application.
