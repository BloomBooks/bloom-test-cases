# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1) Launch a new browser tab.

2)  Navigate to “https://alpha.bloomlibrary.org/” .

**Actions Steps**

1. Verify that the Bloom Library Page appears.
2. Click on the READ tab.
3. Verify that the URL updates to https://alpha.bloomlibrary.org/read.
4. Verify that the READ tab is selected.
5. Verify that the “Search for books” box is visible.
6. Click inside the “Search for books” box.
7. Type “Turka”.
8. Press the Enter key on the keyboard.
9. Verify that the “Turka Books” page appears.
10. Verify that the URL updates to https://alpha.bloomlibrary.org/language:tuz.
11. Click inside the “Search for books” box again.
12. Type “sign language”.
13. Click the Search button in the “Search for books” box.
14. Verify that the “Sign Language Books” page appears.
15. Verify that the URL updates to https://alpha.bloomlibrary.org/sign-language.
16. Click on the READ tab.
17. Verify that the Bloom Library page appears.
18. Open Notepad.
19. Type the word “français”.
20. Select the word “français”.
21. Press Ctrl + C on the keyboard to copy it.
22. Switch back to the Bloom Library page.
23. Click inside the “Search for books” box.
24. Press Ctrl + V on the keyboard to paste the copied text.
25. Verify that the word “français” appears in the “Search for books” box.
26. Click the Search button in the “Search for books” box.
27. Verify that the “français (French) Books” page appears.
28. Verify that the URL updates to https://alpha.bloomlibrary.org/language:fr.

**Cleanup Steps**

1) Close the “https://alpha.bloomlibrary.org/” tab in the web browser.
