# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click The "Moon and the Cap" book from the "Sample Shells" pane.
2. Verify that the "Moon and the Cap" book is displayed in the "Right pane".
3. Click the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.
4. Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.
5. Click the “Publish” tab.
6. Verify that the page navigates to the publish page.
7. Click the 'PDF & Print' button.
8. In the CMYK drop-down list, select “U.S. Web Coated (SWOP) v2”.
9. Click the 'Simple' button  under the “Booklet Mode".
10. Verify that a PDF file is generated.
11. Verify that the generated PDF preview is displayed in the middle pane.
12. Verify that the pages and pictures in the PDF look reasonable and do not have obvious problems.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete ‘The Moon and the Cap’ book.
2. Close Bloom application.
