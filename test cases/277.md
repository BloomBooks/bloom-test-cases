# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Install Annapurna SIL font from “https://software.sil.org/annapurna/download/”
2. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click The "Moon and the Cap" book from the "Sample Shells" pane.
2. Verify that the "Moon and the Cap" book is displayed in the "Right pane".
3. Click the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.
4. Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.
5. Click " Page 2" from the "Pages" pane.
6. Verify that the "Page 2" is displayed in the "Right pane".
7. Click on the text box from page 2.
8. Click the "Gear" icon which appears at the bottom left corner of the Page in "Right Pane".
9. Verify that the "Format" dialog box is displayed..
10. Click the "Characters" tab.
11. Click the Font "Dropdown" menu.
12. Verify the newly installed "Annapurna" font appears in the menu.
10. Click the "Annapurna SIL " font.
11. Verify that the text font is changed to "Annapurna SIL" font.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Remove Annapurna SIL font.
2. Close Bloom application.
