# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch ‘alpha.bloomlibrary.org’ site.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the PRATHAM BOOKS thumbnail under the 'Publishers' section
2. Verify that the page is navigated to the Pratham books page.
3. Click 'Goodnight' Tinku!' book thumbnail under the 'Pratham Books - Animal Stories'
4. Verify that the page is navigated to the READ page.
5. Click the Back button from the browser.
6. Verify the Pratham books page opens.
7. Click the Back button from the browser.
8. Verify the Home page opens.
9. Click the Little Zebra Books thumbnail under the 'Publishers' section
10. Verify the book is navigated to the Little Zebra books page.
11. Click the 'Everyone is different' book thumbnail under the 'English - Simple Books' section
12. Verify the book is navigated to the READ page.
13. Click the 'Home' link in the breadcrumb navigation
14. Verify that the page is navigated to the Home page.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close ‘alpha.bloomlibrary.org’ site.
