# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the Settings button.
2. Verify the Settings dialog opens.
3. Click the Book Making tab.
4. Click the 'Special Script Settings' link button.
5. Verify the Special Script Settings dialog opens.
6. Click 'This script is written right to left like Arabic, Hebrew, and Urdu' check box to get enabled.
7. Click the OK button.
8. Click the Restart button.
9. Verify Bloom gets restarted.
10. Click the 'A Family Learns about Immunisations' book.
11. Verify the book opens in the Edit mode.
12. Click Page 1 thumbnail.
13. Verify Page 1 appears on the right pane.
14. Verify the texts appear in the Right to Left direction in the text box.
15. Click the Publish tab.
16. Click the ePUB button.
17. Verify Publish as ePUB page opens.
18. Click the Preview button.
19. Verify the book opens in the Readium Viewer.
20. Click the Next button.
21. Verify the text appears from right to left.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete ‘A Family Learn about Immunisations’ book.
2. Close bloom application.
