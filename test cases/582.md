# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch “dev.bloomlibrary.org " in any web browser.
2. Install the Bloom app

**Actions Steps**

1. Click the “READ" tab.
2. Verify the “Bloom Library” page opens.
3. Click the “French” book thumbnail.
4. Verify “français (French) books” page opens.
5. Click 'mc f' book thumbnail under Level 1.
6. Verify 'mc f' book opens in the READ page.
7. Verify that the three languages—Spanish, French, and Portuguese—appear as hyperlink text.
8. Verify that a hyperlink is provided for the text "books that may be translations" below  the Languages
9. Verify that a hyperlink is provided for the text 'CC-BY' under the 'License' label at the bottom.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close alpha.bloomlibary.org
