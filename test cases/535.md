# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click 'A Family Learns about Immunisations' shell book under Sample Shells.
2. Click the 'Make a book using this Source' button.
3. Verify the book open in the Edit mode.
4. Click the Collections tab.
5. Right click on the 'A Family Learns about Immunisations' book thumbnail.
6. Verify a submenu opens.
7. Click the 'Duplicate Book' option.
8. Verify a duplicate book is created and appears under the Book Collection.
9. Click on the duplicated book thumbnail.
10. Hover the mouse over the information (i) icon which appears at the top right corner of the Book Preview pane.
11. Verify the Duplicate book name (eg., A Family Learns about Immunisations- Copy) should fully be visible on the Information message.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete ‘A Family Learns about Immunisations’ book.
2. Delete the copy of the Book. (A Family Learns about Immunisations- Copy).
3. Close Bloom application.
