# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click 'The Moon and the Cap' book.
2. Verify that the "Moon and the Cap" book is displayed in the "Right pane".
3. Click the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.
4. Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.
5. Click the ‘Add Page’ button at the bottom left of the page.
6. Verify that the “Add Page..” dialog box is displayed.
7. Select the “Basic Text & Picture” page under the “Basic Pages” option.
8. Click the “ADD PAGE” button.
9. Verify that the “Add Page..” dialog box is closed.
10. Verify that the new page is added.
11. Click the hamburger menu in the top right corner.
12. Verify that the “Talking Book Tool” menu is displayed.
13. Click the “More..” button under the “Talking Book Tool” menu.
14. Verify that more options were displayed.
15. Click the “Overlay Tools” checkbox.
16. Verify the Overlay Tools menu option displays.
17. Click the Speech bubble.
18. Drag and drop the speech bubble to the picture pane.
19. Click the text box area.
20. Verify the text box gets selected.
21. Type 'This is for testing purpose' in the text box.
22. Verify that the typed text appeared in the text box area.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the Moon and the Cap book.
2. Close Bloom application.
