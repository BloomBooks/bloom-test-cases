# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom Reader-alpha in the Android Mobile.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the 'Get more books from our library' option.
2. Verify Bloom Library page opens.
3. Click the 'English 'English' thumbnail under Favorite languages.
4. Verify 'English books' page opens.
5. Click 'Human Body 2 Phase 1..' Talking book.
6. Verify "DOWNLOAD' / 'READ ONLINE' page appears.
7. Click the DOWNLOAD button.
8. Verify 'Downloading book..' message appears for a while.
9. Verify the "Book download is complete' message appears.
10. Verify 'READ NOW' link button appears at the bottom right corner of the page.
11. Click the 'READ NOW' button.
12. Verify the book opens in the Bloom Player.
13. Close the Bloom player.
14. Exit from the bloom library.
15. Open the Bloom Reader application.
16. Verify the downloaded book (thumbnail) appears on the home page.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close alpha.bloomlibary.org
2. Delete the 'Human Body 2 Phase 1..' Talking book.
3. Close Bloom Reader application.
