# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click 'The Moon and the Cap' shell book under Sample Shells.
2. Click MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button.
3. Verify the book opens in the edit mode.
4. Click the Collections tab.
5. Verify the Collections view appears.
6. Right click on the 'The Moon and the Cap' book thumbnail under Book collections.
7. Verify a context menu opens.
8. Click Show File in explorer.
9. Verify the book opens in a directory. (eg., C:\Users\UserName\Documents\Bloom\English Books)
10. Double click on the 'The Moon and the Cap' book folder name.
11. Verify the folder opens.
12. Delete The Moon and the Cap.html file.
13. Close the window.
14. Close Bloom application.
15. Restart Bloom application.
16. Verify a warning message displayed on the Bloom.
17. Click the Close button.
18. Verify the book is recovered and appeared on the Collections.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete ‘The Moon and the Cap’ book.
2. Close Bloom application.
