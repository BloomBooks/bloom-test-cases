# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application

**Action Steps**

1. Click 'The Moon and the Cap' Shell book under Sample Shell books.
2. Verify the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button appears on the Right side pane.
3. Click on the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.
4. Verify the Edit Pane appears.
5. Click Page 1 thumbnail.
6. Verify that page 1 opens in the Edit view.
7. Verify the Source bubble appears on the top right corner of the text box.
8. Click the Hamburger button.
9. Verify the Talking Book Tool menu opens.
10. Verify the text in the text box is highlighted.
11. Verify the Source bubble still appears on the top right corner of the text box.
12. Click on Page 5 thumbnail.
13. Verify the page 5 opens in the Edit view.
14. Verify the text in the text box is highlighted.
15. Verify the Source bubble appears on the top right corner of the text box.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete ‘The Moon and Cap’  book.
2. Close Bloom application.
