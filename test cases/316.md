# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the 'Leveled Reader' book under Specialized Templates heading. (which appears at the lower left pane in Collectins view)
2. Click MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button.
3. Verify the book opens in the Edit mode.
4. Click the Add Page button.
5. Verify the Add Page dialog opens.
6. Click the 'Just text' template from Basic Pages.
7. Click the Add Page button.
8. Verify 'Just text' page is added.
9. Click on the text box.
10. Type 'There' in the text box.
11. Verify the Word count appears '1' on the Leveled Reader Tool menu.
12. Type 'Once' in the text box.
13. Verify the Word count appears '2' on the Leveled Reader Tool menu.
14. Type 'was a boy' in the text box.
15. Verify the Word count appears '5' on the Leveled Reader Tool menu.
16. Click the triangle button near the Page 1 thumbnail.
17. Verify a popup menu opens.
18. Click the 'Remove Page' option.
19. Verify a warning message 'Really Remove Page?' appears in the dialog.
20. Click the 'Remove' button.
21. Verify the Word count Status is changed to '0'.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the Leveled Reader book.
2. Close Bloom application.
