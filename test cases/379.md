# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.
2. Click the Settings button.
3. Verify the Settings dialog opens.
4. Click the Bloom Subscription tab.
5. Verify the Bloom Subscription tab opens.
6. Enter 'Test-727011-1339' in the Subscription code.
7. Verify the Restart button appears instead of the OK button.
8. Click the Restart button.
9. Verify Bloom gets restarted.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the Settings button.
2. Verify the Settings dialog opens.
3. Click the 'Change' link button under Local Language.
4. Verify Choose Language dialog opens.
5. Enter 'Naskapi' in the Search box.
6. Verify Naskapi language bar appears.
7. Click the Naskapi language bar.
8. Verify 'Latin' Script box appears.
9. Click 'Latin' Script box
10. Verify 'Naskapi' name appears under 'Display this language this way' label name.
11. Verify 'nsk-Latn' appears in the 'Customize' button.
12. Click the OK button.
13. Verify 'Naskapi (nsk-Latn)' appears under Local Language name.
14. Click the Restart button.
15. Verify Bloom gets restarted.
16. Click the Settings button.
17. Verify the Settings dialog opens.
18. Verify 'Naskapi (nsk-Latn)' appears under Local Language name.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the Subscription code.
2. Close Bloom application.
