# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch alpha.bloomlibrary.org  in any web browser.
2. Login users using a Moderator Account.

**Actions Steps**

1. Click the French book thumbnail
2. Verify that the French Books page opens
3. Select any book thumbnail ( Eg: 'mc f' book )
4. Verify that the selected  book opens in the READ page
5. Click the 'Draft' checkbox under 'You have permission to modify this book' section
6. Verify that the 'Draft' overlay symbol appears in the book
7. Scroll down the page
8. Click the 'Is a Rebrand' check box located inside the 'You have staff permission on this book' section
9. Click the 'Hide this Book from Everyone' check box
10. Verify an orange color appears on the check box and its content
11. Verify that the 'Save' button is disabled

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close alpha.bloomlibrary.org
