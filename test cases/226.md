# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the "Settings" button from the toolbar.
2. Verify that the "Settings" dialog box is displayed.
3. Click the "Book Making" tab.
4. Verify that the page navigates to the "Book Making" tab.
5. Click the "Special Script Settings" under the "Default Font for English" field.
6. Verify that the "Special Script Settings" dialog box is displayed.
7. Check the check box near 'This script is written right to left like Arabic, Hebrew, and Urdu.
8. Click the OK button.
9. Verify that the "Special Script Settings" dialog box is closed.
10. Click the Restart button.
11. Verify Bloom gets restarted.
12. Click on "The Moon and the Cap” from the Sample Shells.
13. Verify that the preview of “The Moon and the Cap”  opens in the right pane.
14. Click the “MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE” button.
15. Verify that “The Moon and the Cap” opens in edit mode.
16. Select page 1.
17. Verify that the texts were displayed from Right to Left direction.
18. Click on the "text" area.
19. Type 'this is for testing' in the "text" area
20. Verify the cursor moves from Right to Left direction.
21. Select page 2.
22. Verify that the texts were displayed from Right to Left direction.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the “The Moon and the Cap” book.
2. Close Bloom application
