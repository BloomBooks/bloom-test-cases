# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application

**Action Steps**

1. Click a basic book from the left pane.
2. Click the “MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE” button.
3. Verify that the book is displayed in the “Edit” view.
4. Click the “Credits page” from the “Pages” pane.
5. Verify that the “Credits page” is displayed in the “Right  pane”.
6. Click the “Click to Edit Copyrights & License” hint bubble.
7. Verify that the “Copyright and License” dialog box is displayed.
8. Enter “Tester” in the Copyright Holder text box in the “COPYRIGHT” tab.
9. Click the “LICENSE” tab.
10. Click the  “Anyone can use it however they want” radio button.
11. Verify that the “OK” button is enabled.
12. Click the  “Everyone is allowed to” radio button.
13. Verify that the “OK” button is enabled.
14. Click the  “No one can modify or copy…” radio button.
15. Verify that the “OK” button is enabled.
16. Click the “Other” radio button.
17. Verify the “OK” button is disabled.
18. Enter “All rights reserved” in the Other dialog box.
19. Verify the OK button is enabled.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close Bloom application.
