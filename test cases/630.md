# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the Settings button.
2. Verify the Settings dialog opens.
3. Click the Bloom Subscription tab.
4. Enter Sample-361770-1709 in the Subscription code.(expired code)
5. Click the Restart button.
6. Verify the Bloom gets restarted.
7. Click the Settings button.
8. Verify the Settings dialog opens.
9. Click the Book Making tab.
10. Set Bloom Library Bookshelf to something like 'Sample-bookshelf-1'.
11. Click the Restart button.
12. Verify the Bloom gets restarted.
13. Click the Book Making tab.
14. Verify the Bloom Library Bookshelf is set to 'None'.
15. Click the Bloom Subscription tab.
16. Enter 'Test-727011-1339' in the Subscription code.(active code)
17. Click the Restart button.
18. Verify the Bloom gets restarted.
19. Click the Settings button.
20. Verify the Settings dialog opens.
21. Click the Book Making tab.
22. Verify the Bloom Library Bookshelf is set to 'Test-727011-1339'. (the same code)

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the Subscription code.
2. Close Bloom application.
