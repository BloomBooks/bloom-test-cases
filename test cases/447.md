# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the 'Leveled Reader' template under Specialized Templates heading. (which appears at the lower left pane in Collectins view)
2. Click MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button.
3. Verify the book opens in the Edit mode.
4. Click the Add Page button.
5. Click the 'Just text' template from the Basic Pages heading.
6. Click the Add Page button.
7. Verify 'Just text' page is added
8. Click on the text box.
9. Enter a sentence  ‘There once was a boy who grew bored while watching over the village sheep. He wanted to make things more exciting.’ in the text box.
10. Verify the word count shows ‘21’ in the right most pane.
11. Click on the sentence in the text box.
12. Double click on the word ‘exciting’.
13. Verify the word ‘exciting’ got selected.
14. Type ‘beautiful’.
15. Press the Enter key.
16. Verify the word count should not be changed.
17. Verify the word count still shows ‘21’ in the right most pane.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the Leveled Reader book.
2. Close Bloom application.
