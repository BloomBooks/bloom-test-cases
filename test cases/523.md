# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Download any mp3 audio file onto your computer and rename it “Test.mp3”.
2. Launch Bloom application to collection with pro subscription.

Action Steps
1. Click Basic Book under Sources for New Books in the left pane.
2. Verify that the MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button appears in the right pane.
3. Click the MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button.
4. Verify that the Basic Book opens in Edit Mode.
5. Click the Add Page + button.
6. Verify that the Add Page… dialog appears.
7. Select the Just a Picture template from Basic Pages.
8. Click ADD PAGE.
9. Verify that the Page 1 layout is displayed in the right pane.
10. Click the Hamburger menu (top-right corner).
11. Verify that the Talking Book Tool appears.
12. Click the More… button.
13. Verify that the Canvas Tool checkbox is unchecked by default.
14. Check the Canvas Tool checkbox.
15. Verify that the Canvas Tool section expands.
16. Verify that the Bubble button is displayed in the Canvas Tool.
17. Drag and drop the Bubble button from the Canvas Tool onto Page 1.
18. Verify that a new empty Bubble box appears on Page 1.
19. Verify that the ellipsis (More Menu) button appears with the Bubble box.
20. Click the ellipsis (More Menu) button.
21. Verify that the Format text option appears.
22. Click on the Format text option.
23. Verify the Format text dialog appears.
24. Click on the Style Name tab.
25. Verify the Style dropdown appears.
26. Verify the Bubble display on the Style dropdown by default.
27. Click on the Style dropdown.
28. Verify the Style options get listed.
29. Select Big Words.
30. Verify the Style changes from "Bubble" to "Big Words" in the Style dropdown.
31. Click the outside of the Format dialog.
32. Verify the Format dialog closes.
33. Click on the Bubble box.
34. Type "Test".
35. Click on the Talking Tool from the right side pane.
36. Verify the Text "Test" highlighted on Page 1.
37. Click and hold the Speak button.
38. Record the text.
39. Release the Speak button.
40. Verify the Check button becomes enabled.
41. Click the Check button.
42. Verify the appropriate record plays.
43. Click on the Canvas Tool from the right side pane.
44. Verify the highlight on the Text disappears on Page 1.
45. Verify the Style dropdown from the Canvas Tool displays "Speech" by default.
46. Verify the Show Tail check box from the Canvas Tool displays checked by default.
47. Verify the Text Color from the Canvas Tool displays Default for style by default.
48. Verify the Background color from the Canvas Tool displays white by default.
49. Verify the Outer Outline Color dropdown from the Canvas Tool displays None by default.
50. Click the ellipsis (More Menu) button from the Bubble box in Page 1.
51. Verify that the More Menu options appear.
52. Verify that Auto height is selected by default.
53. Click outside the More Menu options.
54. Verify that the More Menu options disappear.
55. Click the Style dropdown from the Canvas Tool.
56. Verify the Style options are listed.
57. Select Caption.
58. Verify the Style "Caption" displays on the Style dropdown.
59. Verify the bubble box changes into the Caption box on Page 1.
60. Verify the Show Tail check box becomes unchecked from the Canvas Tool.
61. Check the Show Tail check box.
62. Verify the Show Tail check box becomes checked from the Canvas Tool.
63. Verify the Tail added to the Caption box from Page 1.
64. Click on the Text Color box from the Canvas Tool.
65. Verify the Text Color dialog box appears.
66. Select the red color on the Text Color dialog box.
67. Click the OK button.
68. Verify the red color displays in the Text Color box in the Canvas Tool.
69. Verify the text in the Caption box on Page 1 is red.
70. Click on the Background Color box from the Canvas Tool.
71. Verify the Background Color dialog box appears.
72. Select the shade of yellow color.
73. Click the OK button.
74. Verify the shade of yellow color displays in the Background Color box in the Canvas Tool.
75. Verify the background color of the Caption box on Page 1 is yellow.
76. Verify the Outer Outline Color dropdown is disabled state in the Canvas Tool.
77. Click the ellipsis (More Menu) button from the Caption box in Page 1.
78. Verify that the More Menu options appear.
79. Verify that Auto height is selected by default.
80. Click the Auto height option.
81. Verify that the More Menu options disappear.
82. Click the ellipsis (More Menu) button again.
83. Verify that the More Menu options appear.
84. Verify that Auto height is now unchecked.
85. Click outside the More Menu options.
86. Verify that the More Menu options disappear.
87. Double-click right after the final “t” in the Caption text box.
88. Verify the cursor directly follows the final “t” in the Caption text box.
89. Press Enter.
90. Hover the cursor over the Caption text box.
91. Verify that the alert "This box has more text than will fit" appears.
92. Click the ellipsis (More Menu) button from the Caption box.
93. Verify that the More Menu options appear.
94. Click the Duplicate option.
95. Verify that the Caption box is duplicated on Page 1.
96. Hover the cursor over the duplicated Caption box.
97. Verify that the alert "This box has more text than will fit" appears.
98. Click the ellipsis (More Menu) button from the duplicated Caption box in Page 1.
99. Verify that the More Menu options appear.
100. Verify that Auto height is unchecked.
101. Click outside the More Menu options.
102. Verify that the More Menu options disappear.
103. Verify the Show Tail check box displays as checked from the Canvas Tool.
104. Verify the red color displays in the Text Color box in the Canvas Tool.
105. Verify the shade of yellow color displays in the Background Color box in the Canvas Tool.
106. Verify the Outer Outline Color dropdown displays as disabled state from the Canvas Tool.
107. Click on the Talking Tool from the right side pane.
108. Verify the duplicated Caption box Text "Test" highlighted on the Page 1.
109. Verify the Check button becomes enabled.
110. Click the Check button.
111. Verify the appropriate record plays.
112. Click on the Canvas Tool from the right side pane.
113. Verify the highlight on the text disappears on Page 1.
114. Click the Add Page + button.
115. Verify that the Add Page… dialog appears.
116. Select the Just a Picture template from Basic Pages.
117. Click ADD PAGE.
118. Verify that Page 2 is added in the left pane.
119. In the Canvas Tool, click the Video icon.
120. Drag and drop the Video icon onto the page.
121. Verify that the empty video overlay icon is displayed.
122. Verify that the Record Yourself button appears below the video overlay.
123. Click the Record Yourself button.
124. Verify that a camera permission alert appears (only the first time).
125. Click Allow.
126. Verify that the alert disappears.
127. Verify that the Sign Language Tool appears on the right pane.
128. Verify that the camera feed is active in the Sign Language Tool.
129. Verify that the red Record button is displayed.
130. Click the red Record button.
131. Verify that recording begins.
132. Record the video.
133. Click the Stop button.
134. Verify that recording stops.
135. Verify that the recorded video appears in the Page 1 video overlay.
136. Hover over the Video overlay box.
137. Click the Play button on the video overlay.
138. Verify that the video starts playing.
139. Click the Pause button.
140. Verify that the video pauses playing.
141. Verify the Trim control button appears on the Canvas Tool.
142. Trim the video using the trim buttons.
143. Click the Play button on the video overlay.
144. Verify that the video is playing the trimmed section only.
145. Click the ellipsis (More Menu) button from the Video overlay box.
146. Verify that the More Menu options appear.
147. Click Duplicate Option.
148. Verify that the Video overlay box is duplicated on Page 2.
149. Hover over the duplicated Video overlay box.
150. Click the Play button on the duplicated Video overlay box.
151. Verify that the video plays the trimmed section only.
152. Click the Add Page + button.
153. Verify that the Add Page… dialog appears.
154. Select the Custom Game page under Games Pages.
155. Click ADD PAGE.
156. Verify that Page 3 is added in the left pane.
157. Verify the Game Tool displays on the right side.
158. Drag the image overlay icon from the "Object that does not Move" section.
159. Drop it on Page 3.
160. Verify the image overlay box is placed on Page 3.
161. Click the ellipsis (More Menu) button from the image overlay box.
162. Verify that the More Menu options appear.
163. Click the "Choose image from your computer..." option.
164. Verify the "Image tool box" dialog appears.
165. Click the File icon.
166. Verify the Choose the Picture file dialog appears.
167. Select desired image.
168. Click the Open button.
169. Verify the Choose the Picture file dialog closes.
170. Verify the selected image displays on the Image tool box dialog.
171. Click the “Crop” button on the "Image Toolbox" dialog box.
172. Crop the image into half using the adjusters.
173. Click the “OK” button.
174. Verify that the "Image Toolbox" dialog box is closed.
175. Verify that the cropped image is added in the image overlay box.
176. Click the ellipsis (More Menu) button from the image overlay box.
177. Verify that the More Menu options appear.
178. Click the "Set image information..." option.
179. Verify that the “Copyright & License” dialog box opens.
180. Click on the COPYRIGHT tab.
181. Type "Test" in the Illustrator/Photographer field.
182. Enter “1500” in the Copyright Year field.
183. Type “Test Copyright Holder” in the “Copyright Holder” field.
184. Click the “OK” button.
185. Verify that the “Copyright & License” dialog box closes.
186. Verify the alert box "Copy this information to all other pictures in this book?" appears.
187. Click the YES button.
188. Verify the alert box "Copy this information to all other pictures in this book?" closes.
189. Click the ellipsis (More Menu) button on the image overlay box.
190. Verify that the More Menu options are displayed.
191. Verify that "None" is shown on the "This sound will play when the item is touched" right arrow button.
192. Click the right arrow button.
193. Verify that the "Choose..." option appears.
194. Click on the Choose.. option.
195. Verify the Choose sound file dialog appears.
196. Select audio “Test.mp3”.
197. Click the Open button.
198. Verify that the "Choose Sound File" dialog closes.
199. Verify that the selected audio plays.
200. Click the ellipsis (More Menu) button on the image overlay box.
201. Verify that the More Menu options are displayed.
202. Verify that "Test.mp3" is shown on the "This sound will play when the item is touched" option.
203. Click the Duplicate option.
204. Verify that the image overlay box is duplicated on Page 3.
205. Verify that the cropped image displays in the duplicated image overlay box.
206. Click the ellipsis (More Menu) button on the duplicated image overlay box.
207. Verify that the More Menu options are displayed.
208. Verify that “Test.mp3" is shown on the "This sound will play when the item is touched" option.
209. Click the "Set image information..." option.
210. Verify that the “Copyright & License” dialog box opens.
211. Click on the COPYRIGHT tab.
212. Verify "Test" appears in the Illustrator/Photographer field.
213. Verify “1500” appears in the Copyright Year field.
214. Verify “Test Copyright Holder” appears in the “Copyright Holder” field.
215. Click the CANCEL button.
216. Verify that the “Copyright & License” dialog box closes.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the "Test" book.
2. Close the Bloom application.
