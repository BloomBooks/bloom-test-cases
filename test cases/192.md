# Steps

**Setup**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click The "Basic Book" book from the "Source For New Books" pane.
2. Verify that the preview of the "Basic Book" book is displayed in the "Right pane".
3. Click the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.
4. Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.
5. Verify that the "Front Cover" page is displayed.
6. Type "Title 1" in the title text box.
7. Click the Hamburger menu from the top right corner.
8. Verify the Talking Book Tool appears.
9. Hold the speak button from "Section 3" and speak "Title 1".
10. Verify that the audio "Title 1" is audible.
11. Click the ‘Add Page’ button at the bottom left pane.
12. Verify that the “Add Page..” dialog box is displayed.
13. Select the “Just a Picture” page under the “Basic Pages” option.
14. Click the “ADD PAGE” button.
15. Verify that the “Add Page..” dialog box is closed.
16. Verify that the new page (Page 1) is displayed.
17. Click the Hamburger menu from the top right corner.
18. Verify the Talking Book Tool appears.
19. Verify that the "Section 3 (Speak)" button is disabled.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the basic book.
