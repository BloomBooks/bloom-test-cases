# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the Basic Book.
2. Click the 'Make a book using this Source' button.
3. Verify the book open in the Edit mode.
4. Click the Add page button.
5. Verify the Add page dialog opens.
6. Click 'Just a Picture' template.
7. Click the Add page button.
8. Verify the 'Just a Picture' page is added.
9. Click the 3 lines button which appears at the top right corner of the Bloom Editor.
10. Verify the More option menu opens.
11. Click the 'Overlay tool' option check box.
12. Verify the Overlay Tool menu opens.
13. Drag the Video Overlay on the picture page.
14. Drop the Video Overlay on the picture page.
15. Verify the Video Overlay is placed over the picture.
16. Click the 'Record Yourself..' button on the Video overlay.
17. Verify the Video preview appears on the right side of the page.
18. Click the 'Record' button.
19. Verify that it starts video recording.
20. Click the Stop button.
21. Verify the video has been recorded.
22. Click any corner of the overlay.
23. Drag it to any corner to resize the overlay.
24. Verify the aspect ratio of video is maintained while resizing (larger or smaller).
25. Verify no gaps appear on the edges of the container.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the Basic book.
2. Close Bloom application.
