# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch alpha.bloomlibrary.org
2. Click on the Profile icon at the top right corner
3. Sign in to bloom by clicking on the 'Sign In/Sign Up' button

**Action Steps**

1. Click the Profile icon located at the top-right corner
2. Verify that the My Books option is displayed
3. Click 'My Books' option
4. Verify that the 'My Books' page opens
5. Verify that the 'Draft' overlay appears on all the books
6. Click any one of the book from the 'My Books' page
7. Verify that the selected book opens in the Read page
8. Verify that the 'Download into Bloom for Editing' button appears in 'You have permission to modify this book' section
9. Verify that the Which Format Buttons to Show for this Book dropdown is displayed under the Formats section
10. Click on the 'Which Format Buttons to Show for this Book' dropdown
11. Verify that the list of formatting options appears in the dropdown
12. Verify that the Staff Controls button is not displayed
13. Verify that the Raw Book Data button is not displayed
14. Verify that the Delete button is displayed at the bottom of the section

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close alpha.bloomlibrary.org
