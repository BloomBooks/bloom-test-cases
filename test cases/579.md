# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch “alpha.bloomlibrary.org" in any web browser.
2. Install the Bloom app

**Actions Steps**

1. Click the “READ" tab.
2. Verify the Bloom Library page opens.
3. Click the 'English' thumbnail.
4. Verify “English books” page opens.
5. Click the right-end scroll arrow in the "Level 1" section.
6. Click the "See more of these books" thumbnail.
7. Verify that the page redirects to the "Level 1" page.
8. Click the “At the Market” book thumbnail under "English - Level 1 - Business".
9. Verify “At the Market” book opens on the READ page.
10. Click the 'Translate into your language - Download into Bloom Editor'  text button present on the right side.
11. Verify a message 'Almost there..' appears with a checkbox button.
12. Click the check box  for 'I get it. Don't show me this again' .
13. Click the 'Download Book' button.
14. Verify 'Open Bloom?' A warning message appears in a dialog.
15. Click the check box in the dialog box.
16. Click the 'Open Bloom' button.
17. Verify the progress bar 'Downloading book' appears on the screen.
18. Verify 'Download complete' dialog appears with the OK button.
19. Click the OK button.
20. Verify the downloaded book opens in the Bloom application.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close alpha.bloomlibary.org
