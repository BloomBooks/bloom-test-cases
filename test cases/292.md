# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click The "Moon and the Cap" book from the "Sample Shells" pane.
2. Verify that the "Moon and the Cap" book is displayed in the "Right pane".
3. Click the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.
4. Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.
5. Click the “Title Page” under the “Pages” pane.
6. Verify that the pink bubbles are correctly positioned on the right side of the page.
7. Verify that text is not cut off at the bottom of the pink bubble.
8. Click the “Credits Page” under the “Pages” pane.
9. Verify that the pink bubbles are correctly positioned on the right side of the page.
10. Verify that text is not cut off at the bottom of the pink bubble.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the Moon and the Cap book.
2. Close Bloom application.
