# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch the Bloom application

**Action Steps**

1. Verify that the ‘Sources for New Books’ section appeared on the left pane
2. Click the "A Family Learns about Immunisations" book under the "Sample Shells" section from the Collections tab.
3. Verify that the template preview is opened.
4. Click on the ‘MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE’ button.
5. Verify that the template is opened in the edit tab.
6. Click the "Page 1" thumbnail from the "Pages" pane.
7. Drag the "Page 1" thumbnail icon on the Page 4 thumbnail icon.
8. Verify that the "Page 4" thumbnail icon is reordered to the "Page 1" thumbnail icon.
9. Verify that the "Page 1" thumbnail icon is reordered to the "Page 4" thumbnail icon.
10. Click the Collections tab.
11. Click the Settings button in the top right corner.
12. Verify that the Settings dialog opens.
13. Click the "Book Making" tab.
14. Verify that the "Front/Back Matter Pack" pane appears on the right side of the dialog.
15. Click the "Paper Saver" option.
16. Drag the "SIL-PNG" option downward.
17. Verify that the drag feature is not allowed in this Xmatter dialog.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the A Family Learns about Immunisations book.
2. Close the Bloom application.
