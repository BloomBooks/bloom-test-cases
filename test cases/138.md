# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application

**Action Steps**

1. Click the Settings button.
2. Verify the Settings dialog opens.
3. Click the Book Making tab.
4. Click the Page Number Style dropdown list box.
5. Select 'Tamil'
6. Click the Restart button.
7. Verify bloom gets restarted.
8. Click 'The Moon and the Cap' shell book under Sample Shells.
9. Click MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button.
10. Verify the book opens in the edit mode.
11. Click Page 1.
12. Verify that the page number appears in Tamil at the bottom of the page.
13. Click the Publish tab.
14. Click the PDF & Print button.
15. Click the Simple mode button.
16. Verify the PDF book is created.
17. Navigate to page 1 or 2 (in the pdf book).
18. Verify that the page number appears in Tamil at the bottom of the page.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete ‘The Moon and the Cap’ book.
2. Close Bloom application.
