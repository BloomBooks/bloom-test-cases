# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click a Template Starter book.
2. Click the 'Make a book using this Source' button.
3. Verify it opens in the edit mode.
4. Click the Add Page button.
5. Verify the Add page dialog opens.
6. Click the 'Custom' template.
7. Click the Add page button.
8. Verify the Custom template is added.
9. Verify the Corner of the pages appears with a dashed line border.
10. Click the Collections tab.
11. Verify the Custom Template book thumbnail appears with a dashed line border.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close Bloom application.
