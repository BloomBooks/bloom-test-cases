# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click 'The Moon and the Cap' shell book.
2. Click the 'MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE' button.
3. Verify the book opens in the edit view.
4. Click the A5 Portrait combo box from the tool bar.
5. Verify the page layout list box opens.
6. Select A5 Landscape option.
7. Verify the page layout is changed to A5 Landscape.
8. Click the Publish tab.
9. Click the PDF & Print button.
10. Verify Publish to PDF & Print view opens.
11. Click the Simple mode under Booklet Mode.
12. Verify the PDF book appeared in Landscape mode.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete ‘The Moon and the Cap’ book.
2. Close Bloom application.
