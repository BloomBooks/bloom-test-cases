# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.
2. Open a collection in which the Local Language is English.

**Action Steps**

1. Hover the mouse over the 'English books on BloomLibrary.org' text above the collection name.
2. Verify 'View on BloomLibray.org' blue link button is displayed.
3. Click the 'View on BloomLibrary.org' blue link button.
4. Verify BloomLibrary.org site opens in the browser.
5. Type “grid” in the “search for books” field and press Enter key on keyboard.
6. Verify that “You must log in to see this page.” message is displayed.
7. Click the profile icon from the toolbar.
8. Click the “Sign In/ Sign Up” option from the submenu.
9. Sign in with the valid credential.
10. Verify that the matching books were displayed.
11. Click the dropdown near “All” under the “Level” column.
12. Select '1' in the filter column.
13. Verify that only level 1 books are filtered.
14. Verify that the number 1 is shown in that column for every book.
15. Click the dropdown near “1” under the “Level” column.
16. Select '4' in the filter column.
17. Verify that only level 4 books are filtered.
18. Verify that the number 4 is shown in that column for every book.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close Bloom application.
2. Close the browser window.
