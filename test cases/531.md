# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Enter ‘https://bloomlibrary.org/activities/quiz-activities’ in the browser window.
2. Click any book with a simple quiz.
3. Verify the book opens on a web page.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the READ button.
2. Verify the book opens in the Bloom Player.
3. Navigate to the Quiz page.
4. Click on the answers check box, try to trigger the pages to go forward or backwards by moving the mouse while clicking.
5. Verify the question answer sounds normally and the page does not move forward or backward.
6. Repeat the above steps on a computer with a touch screen.
7. Repeat the above steps on a Tablet or a Mobile device.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close the Quiz book.
2. Close the browser window.
