# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch bloomlibrary.org/stats/bookStatistics  site.

**Action Steps**

1. Click Book Statistics in the dropdown list.
2. Verify the book titles should appear in the Book Statistics page.
3. Click the 'ALL TIME' button which appears at the top right corner of the page.
4. Verify the Calendar page opens.
5. Click 1 Feb 2024 in the 'Include Events From' Calendar.
6. Verify the sorted books appear on the bookstatistic page.
7. Click the 'CSV' button which appears at the bottom of the page.
8. Verify the CSV excel file is successfully downloaded.
9. Double click on the BookStatistics.CSV file.
10. Verify the CSV file opens in the Spreadsheet.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close alpha.bloomlibary.org.
