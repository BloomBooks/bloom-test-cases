# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the Other Collection button.
2. Verify Open/Create Collections dialog opens.
3. Click the 'Create New Collection' button.
4. Verify Create New Bloom Collection dialog opens.
5. Type 'en' in the Search box.
6. Verify the 'English' Language bar appears under the Search box.
7. Click the 'English' Language bar.
8. Verify the 'English' language bar gets selected. (eg., the english language bar appears in blue color)

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close Bloom application.
