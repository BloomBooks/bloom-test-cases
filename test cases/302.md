# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click 'The Moon and the Cap' book.
2. Verify that the "Moon and the Cap" book is displayed in the "Right pane".
3. Click the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.
4. Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.
5. Click on the “Title page”.
6. Click on the text area “Written by Noni Illustrations by Angie and Upesh”.
7. Verify that all the texts were not highlighted in blue.
8. Click on page 2.
9. Click on the text area.
10. Verify that all the texts were not highlighted in blue.
11. Click on page 5.
12. Click on the text area.
13. Verify that all the texts were not highlighted in blue.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete ‘The Moon and the Cap' book.
2. Close Bloom application.
