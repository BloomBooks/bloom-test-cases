# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the Moon and the Cap book.
2. Verify the book opens in the Edit mode.
3. Click the Add Page button.
4. Verify the Add Page dialog opens.
5. Click the 'Just a Picture' template.
6. Verify the 'Just a Picture' template is added.
7. Click the three lines button which appears at the top right corner of the Edit page.
8. Verify the More menu opens.
9. Click the 'Overlay Tool' check box.
10. Verify the Overlay Tool menu opens.
11. Drag and drop the Speech bubble overlay on the picture pane.
12. Verify the Speech overlay appears on the picture pane.
13. Drag the overlay off the page.
14. Verify it should stop at some point.
15. Move it back to the middle without releasing the mouse.
16. Verify the overlay should move it back again in the middle.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the Moon and the Cap book.
2. Close Bloom application.
