# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click a Basic book.
2. Click the 'MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE' button.
3. Verify book opens in the edit view.
4. Click the Add page button.
5. Verify the Add page dialog opens.
6. Click the 'Just text’ template.
7. Click the Add page button.
8. Verify that the page is added.
9. Click on the text box.
10. Enter ‘This is for recording’ in the box.
11. Click the Hamburger button.
12. Verify the TBT menu opens.
13. Verify the entire text is highlighted.
14. Hover the mouse over the Phone icon which appears at the bottom right corner of the desktop.
15. Verify a message saying that 'Bloom <Version> is using your microphone'.
16. Click the Close button of the TBT menu.
17. Verify TBT menu closes.
18. Verify the text is not highlighted.
19. Hover the mouse over the Phone icon which appears at the bottom right corner of the desktop.
20. Verify there is no message saying that 'Bloom is using your microphone'
21. Click the Hamburger button.
22. Verify the TBT menu opens.
23. Verify the entire text is highlighted.
24. Click the Publish tab.
25. Hover the mouse over the Phone icon which appears at the bottom right corner of the desktop.
26. Verify there is no message saying that 'Bloom is using your microphone'

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the Basic Book.
2. Close Bloom application.
