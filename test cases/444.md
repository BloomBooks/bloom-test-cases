# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Enter ‘ مرحبا جون! هل هذا أنت؟ وداعا يوحنا’ in the Notepad.
2. Press Ctrl+A to select all.
3. Press Ctrl+C to copy the Arabic text.
4. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the 'Leveled Reader' template under Specialized Templates heading. (which appears at the lower left pane in Collectins view)
2. Click MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button.
3. Verify the book opens in the Edit mode.
4. Click the Add Page button.
5. Click the 'Just text' template from the Basic Pages heading.
6. Click the Add Page button.
7. Verify 'Just text' page is added.
8. Click on the text box.
9. Press Ctrl+V to paste مرحبا جون! هل هذا أنت؟ وداعا يوحنا. text on the text pane.
10. Verify the Leveled reader should report 7 words, average of 2.3 per sentence.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete Leveled Reader book.
2. Close Bloom application.
