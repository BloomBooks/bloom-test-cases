# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Download an image from web and rename the file name as 'FB_IMG_1629606288606'
2. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Verify that the ‘Sources for New Books’ section appeared on the left pane
2. Click on the ‘Basic book’ from the Templates
3. Verify that the template preview is opened
4. Click on the ‘MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE’ button
5. Verify that the template is opened in the edit mode.
6. Click on the ‘Add page’ button
7. Verify that the ‘Add Pages…’ dialog opened
8. Click on the ‘Just a Picture’  template
9. Click on the ‘Add Page’ button
10. Verify that the ‘Just a Picture’ template is added to the book
11. Click on the ‘Change image’ button
12. Verify that the ‘Image Toolbox’ dialog appeared
13. Click on the ‘File’ button
14. Verify that the ‘Choose Picture File’ dialog opened
15. Select the image ('FB_IMG_1629606288606') downloaded from the web
16. Click on the Open button
17. Click ‘Ok’ button on the ‘Image Toolbox’ dialog
18. Verify that the image is successfully imported to the page in a book.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the basic book.
2. Close Bloom application.
