# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application
2. Click the Settings button.
3. Verify the Settings dialog opens.
4. Click the Bloom Subscription tab.
5. Verify the Bloom Subscription tab opens.
6. Enter 'Test-727011-1339' in the Subscription code.
7. Verify the Restart button appears instead of the OK button.
8. Click the Restart button.
9. Verify Bloom gets restarted.

**Action Steps**

1. Click an eBook.
2. Click MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button.
3. Verify the book opens in the Edit mode.
4. Click the Add Page button.
5. Verify the Add Page dialog box opens.
6. Click the “Order Words”  page.
7. Click the Add Page button.
8. Verify 'Order Words' is added.
9. Click on the Text Block Overlay.
10. Paste 'This is for testing purpose' sentence  in the text box.
11. Verify the overlay resizes to show all of the text.
12. Click on the right side 'Change Shape' button.
13. Drag it towards the right direction.
14. Verify the height of the overlay is immediately snap back to require only the required number lines.
15. Click on the right side 'Change Shape' button.
16. Drag it towards the left direction.
17. Verify the height of the overlay grows as required.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the Subscription code.
2. Delete the eBook.
3. Close Bloom application.
