# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Download Sample Music mp3 file from the given link : https://download.samplelib.com/mp3/sample-6s.mp3 in the download folder.
2. Launch Bloom application
3. Click the Settings button.
4. Verify the Settings dialog opens.
5. Click the Bloom Subscription tab.
6. Verify the Bloom Subscription tab opens.
7. Enter 'Test-727011-1339' in the Subscription code.
8. Verify the Restart button appears instead of the OK button.
9. Click the Restart button.
10. Verify Bloom gets restarted.

**Action Steps**

1. Click an eBook under Templates in the Collections view.
2. Verify MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button appears on the preview pane.
3. Click MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button.
4. Verify the book opens in the Edit mode.
5. Enter 'Music Book' on the Front cover title box.
6. Click the Credits Page.
7. Verify the Copyright and License dialog opens.
8. Enter 'Tester' in the Copyright Holder text box.
9. Click the OK button.
10. Verify the Copyright and License dialog closes.
11. Click the Add Page button.
12. Verify the Add Page dialog opens.
13. Click the 'Basic Text & Picture' template.
14. Click the Add Page button.
15. Verify the 'Basic Text & Picture' is added.
16. Verify the page is added in the edit mode.
17. Click the 'Change Image' button on the picture pane.
18. Verify the Image Toolbox dialog opens.
19. Enter 'Car' in the Search box.
20. Press the Enter key.
21. Verify Car images appear on the preview pane.
22. Click any of the Car images.
23. Click the OK button.
24. Verify the Car image appears on the picture pane.
25. Click on the text pane.
26. Enter ‘This is a Car’ in the text pane.
27. Click the Hamburger button.
28. Verify the TBT tool menu opens.
29. Click the More..option which appears at the bottom of the pane.
30. Verify More..menu opens.
31. Select the Music Tool Check box.
32. Verify the Music Tool menu opens.
33. Select 'Start new music..' radio button.
34. Click the 'Choose...' link button.
35. Verify the Open dialog appears.
36. Click the Downloads from the left pane.
37. Verify the Downloads folder opens.
38. Select 'sample-x.mp3'music file. (eg., sample-6s.mp3)
39. Click the Open button.
40. Verify the music file added in the Music tool.
41. Verify the play button is enabled.
42. Click the Play button.
43. Verify the music plays.
44. Click the Pause button.
45. Verify the music stopped.
46. Click on the text pane on page 1.
47. Click Talking Book Tool option. (which appears at the bottom of the rightmost pane)
48. Verify the entire sentence is highlighted.
49. Press and Hold the Speak button.
50. Record 'This is a Car image'
51. Release the Speak button.
52. Verify the Check button is enabled.
53. Click the Check button.
54. Verify the recorded audio plays.
55. Click the Music Tool option.
56. Decrease the volume to 40% by dragging the white circle button to the left.
57. Click the Publish tab.
58. Click the BloomPUB button.
59. Verify Publish as BloomPUB view opens.
60. Click the Preview button.
61. Verify the book opens in the Preview pane.
62. Click the Next button.
63. Verify Page 1 opens.
64. Verify that the narration is playing at 100% volume, and that the music audio is playing at half the volume of the narration.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete Sample-xx.mp3 file.
2. Delete the Subscription code.
3. Delete Music book..
4. Close Bloom application.
