# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Close the application.
2. Right click on the Bloom icon on the desktop.
3. Click the ‘Run as administrator’ option.
4. Verify a warning message appears on the screen with a Yes or No button.
5. Click the Yes button.
6. Verify the Bloom application open successfully.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close Bloom application.
