# Steps

**SetupSteps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click 'A Family Learns about Immunisations' book thumbnail under Sample Shells heading.
2. Click the 'Make a book using this source' button.
3. Verify the book is open in the Edit mode.
4. Click the triangle button under Page 2 book thumbnail.
5. Verify the popup menu appears.
6. Click the 'Choose different layout' option.
7. Verify 'Choose Different Layout..' dialog appears.
8. Click the 'Picture on Left' template.
9. Click the 'Use this Layout' button.
10. Verify the page layout is changed in the Edit view.
11. Hover mouse over on the text box.
12. Verify the source bubble appears on the text box.
13. Click the outside page.
14. Click inside the text box.
15. Verify the source bubble now appears.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete ‘A Family Learns about Immunisations’ book.
2. Close Bloom application.
