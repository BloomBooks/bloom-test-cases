# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Create a new blank Word document.
2. Type the following text into the document: This is what Lisa Cron lovingly refers to as your “so what factor” and what I call your — why does someone need to read your book slash why should they care about that person you just 4. choose to tell your story about in question 1?
3. Save the document as "test.docx".
4. Close and reopen the previously saved Word document to confirm it was saved properly.
5. Launch the Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Right-click on the Standard toolbar.
2. Verify a popup menu opens.
3. Click 'Start Measuring Performance' from the popup menu.
4. Verify the 'Bloom Performance Measurement' Page opens in a browser.
5. Navigate to the Bloom application.
6. Verify that the ‘Sources for New Books’ section appeared on the left pane.
7. Click the ‘Moon and the Cap' from the Sample Shells in the ‘Source for New Books’ section.
8. Verify that the ‘Moon and the Cap' book preview is opened.
9. Click the ‘MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE’ button.
10. Verify that the ‘Moon and the Cap book’ is opened in the Edit mode.
11. Open the test.docx document.
12. Copy all the text from test.docx.
13. In the Bloom application, select Page 1 in the left-side Pages pane.
14. Click inside the text area on page 1, press Ctrl+A to select all existing text, and then press Ctrl+V to paste the copied text.
15. Verify that the previously existing text is completely replaced with the copied text from test.docx.
16. Verify that all the text is pasted correctly into the text area and memory loss occurs.
17. Navigate to the 'Bloom Performance Measurement' Page opens in a browser.
18. Verify that the performance data (Memory usage in MB/GB and Time in seconds) is recorded and displayed in the Bloom Performance Measurement graph after pasting the text.
19. Navigate to the Bloom application.
20. Select Page 2 in the left-side Pages pane.
21. Click inside the text area on page 2, press Ctrl+A to select all existing text, and then press Ctrl+V to paste the copied text.
22. Verify that all the text is pasted correctly into the text area and memory loss occurs.
23. Navigate to the 'Bloom Performance Measurement' Page opens in a browser.
