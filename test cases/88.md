# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Select the “Wall Calendar" book under the “Specialized Templates”.
2. Verify that the preview of the “Wall Calendar” template is displayed in the "Right pane".
3. Click the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.
4. Verify that the “Setup” dialog box is displayed.
5. Select the year “2026” in the year field.
6. Click the “OK” button.
7. Verify that the “Setup” dialog box is closed.
8. Verify that the book opens in the "Edit" mode.
9. Click the “May” month in the “Pages” pane.
10. Verify that the “May” month is displayed and has six weeks.
11. Verify that all weeks fit within 6 rows and no extra row is created.
12. Verify that there is no UI distortion or cut-off issue.
13. Click the “February” month in the “Pages” pane.
14. Verify that the “February” month is displayed and has four weeks.
15. Verify that all weeks fit within 4 rows and no extra row is created.
16. Verify that there is no UI distortion or cut-off issue.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the ‘Wall Calendar’ book.
2. Close Bloom application.
