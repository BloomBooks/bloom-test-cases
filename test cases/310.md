# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click a basic book.
2. Verify it opens in the edit mode.
3. Click the Add page button.
4. Verify Add page dialog opens.
5. Click the 'Basic Text & Picture' template.
6. Click the Add page button.
7. Verify the Basic Text & Picture template is added.
8. Click the Add page button.
9. Verify Add page dialog opens.
10. Click 'Just a text' template.
11. Click the Add page button.
12. Verify the Just a text template is added.
13. Click page 1. (Basic Text & Picture)
14. Hover the mouse over the picture pane.
15. Verify the Image Editing Buttons are shown.
16. Click the More button.
17. Verify the More options menu opens.
18. Click the Overlay Tool check box.
19. Verify the Overlay Tool menu opens.
20. Drag and drop speech bubble on the picture pane.
21. Hover the mouse over the picture pane.
22. Verify the Image Editing Buttons are hidden.
23. Click outside the speech bubble on the picture pane.
24. Hover the mouse over the picture pane.
25. Verify the Image Editing Buttons are shown.
26. Click Page 2 (just a text)
27. Click Page 1 again.
28. Hover the mouse over the picture pane.
29. Verify the Image Editing Buttons are hidden.
30. Click on the picture pane.
31. Hover the mouse over the picture pane.
32. Verify the Image Editing Buttons are shown.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close Bloom application.
