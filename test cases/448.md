# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Open the Notepad.
2. Enter ‘He saw big, purple, juicy grapes.’ in the Notepad editor.
3. Press Ctrl+A to select all.
4. Press Ctrl+C to copy the entire text.
5. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the 'Leveled Reader' template under Specialized Templates heading. (which appears at the lower left pane in Collectins view)
2. Click MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button.
3. Verify the book opens in the Edit mode.
4. Click the Add Page button.
5. Click the 'Just text' template from the Basic Pages heading.
6. Click the Add Page button.
7. Verify 'Just text' page is added
8. Click on the text box.
9. Type “A hungry fox once looked everywhere for food.” in the text box.
10. Verify the word count shows ‘8’ on the right most pane.
11. Click the text box.
12. Press Ctrl+V to paste the text.
13. Verify the word count increases. (eg., it shows ‘13) on the right most pane.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close the Notepad editor.
2. Delete the Leveled Reader book.
3. Close Bloom application.
