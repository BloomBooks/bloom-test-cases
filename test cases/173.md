# Steps

**Setup**

1. Launch Bloom application
2. Click the Settings button.
3. Verify the Settings dialog opens.
4. Click the Bloom Subscription tab.
5. Verify the Bloom Subscription tab opens.
6. Enter 'Test-727011-1339' in the Subscription code.
7. Verify the Restart button appears instead of the OK button.
8. Click the Restart button.
9. Verify Bloom gets restarted.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the “Basic Book” under the “Templates” area.
2. Verify that the  “Basic Book” template is displayed in the "Right pane".
3. Click the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.
4. Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.
5. Click on the "Add Page" button from the "Pages" section
6. Verify that the "Add Page..." dialog opens
7. Select the "Basic Text & Picture" template from the "Basic Pages"
8. Click the "ADD PAGE" button
9. Verify that Page "1" is added under the Pages section on the left side
10. Verify that the layout of Page "1" is displayed on the right side
11. Hover the mouse over the picture pane in the Page "1" layout
12. Verify that the "Change image" button is displayed
13. Click the "Change image' button in the picture pane
14. Verify the "Image Toolbox" dialog box opens
15. Click the File button.
16. Verify "Choose Picture Files" dialog box opens.
17. Select a valid ".png" image from the "Choose Picture Files" dialog box.
18. Click the Open Key.
19. Verify that the selected image displayed on the "Image Toolbox" dialog box.
20. Click the OK button.
21. Verify that the selected picture displayed in the picture pane
22. Click the Hamburger menu from the top right corner
23. Verify that the "Talking Book Tool" appears
24. Click inside the "Textbox" in the Page "1" layout
25. Type the sentence "Text Box 1: Test Talking Book Tool"
26. Verify the the sentence "Text Box 1: Test Talking Book Tool" is highlighted
27. Click and Hold the "Speak" button in the "Talking Book Tool"
28. Record the phrase “Text Box 1: Test Talking Book Tool”
29. Release the "Speak" button
30. Verify that the "Check" button becomes enabled
31. Click on the Check button
32. Verify that the recorded audio plays correctly
33. Switch on the "Change Layout" toggle button above page "1"
34. Verify that the "Text Box" pane appeared on the page "1"
35. Hover the mouse over the "Text Box" pane
36. Verify the + button appears on the Text box
37. Click on the + button
38. Verify that the “Picture, Video, or Text” options appear
39. Click the "Text" Option
40. Verify that the new "Text Box" added on the Page "1" layout
41. Switch off the "Change Layout" toggle button above page "1"
42. Click on the newly added Textbox in the Page "1" layout
43. Enter the sentence "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit."
44. Click the Enter key
45. Enter the sentence in the next paragraph "Sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua."
46. Verify the the second sentence "Sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua" is highlighted
47. Verify that the Check button in the Talking Book Tool is in a disabled state
48. Click on the Textbox containing the text “Text Box 1: Test Talking Book Tool”
49. Verify that the "Check" button in the "Talking Book Tool" is in enabled state
50. Click on the "Advanced" button in the "Talking Book Tool"
51. Verify that the list of Advanced options appears
52. Click on the text box contains the paragraph
53. Place the cursor between words in the text ( Eg: Between Lorem and ipsum )
54. Select the "Insert Segment Marker" option in the Advanced options
55. Verify that the “|” character is inserted between the words
56. Verify that the "By Whole Text Box" option is selected by default under the "Recording Mode"
57. Click on the "By Sentence" option under "Recording Mode"
58. Verify that only the first paragraph is highlighted in the Text Box
59. Click on the Text Box containing text "Text Box 1: Test Talking Book Tool"
60. Select the "Recording Mode" option as "By Whole Text Box" in the Advanced settings
61. Verify that the entire text in the Text Box is highlighted as a single segment for recording
62. Click the text box 2 containing the paragraph
63. Verify that the "Recording Mode" option is selected as "By Sentence"
64. Enable the "Show image descriptions" options in the Advanced settings
65. Verify that the picture pane splits into two sections as image and text area
66. Enter the description as "Testing the image descriptions feature" in the text area
67. Verify that the entered text highlighted
68. Verify that the "By Sentence" option is selected as default under "Recording Mode"

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the basic book.
2. Close the Bloom application.
