# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Hover the mouse over the 'English books on BloomLibrary.org' hyperlink.
2. Verify 'View on BloomLibray.org' blue link button is displayed.
3. Click the 'View on BloomLibrary.org' blue link button.
4. Verify BloomLibrary.org site opens in the browser.
5. Type “Beginning with God” in the “search for books” field.
6. Click the Enter button.
7. Verify that the books related to the “Beginning with God” are displayed.
8. Select the “1 - Beginning with God Landscape” book.
9. Verify that the “Beginning with God landscape” book is displayed.
10. Click the “READ” button.
11. Verify that the book is opened in the Bloom Player.
12. Click the “Next Page” button.
13. Verify page 1 is displayed.
14. Verify that the audio starts playing.
15. Click the “Pause” button.
16. Verify that the book stops playing.
17. Verify that the scrollbar is fully visible on the right side of page 1.
18. On page 1 use the scrollbar to scroll through the texts.
19. Verify that the page did not slide while scrolling.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close the 1-Beginning with God Landscape book in the browser.
2. Close Bloom application.
