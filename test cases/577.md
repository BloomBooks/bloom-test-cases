# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch ‘alpha.bloomlibrary.org’ site.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the 'English' thumbnail under the 'Find Books By Language' section
2. Verify English books page opens.
3. Click any book thumbnail ( Eg: At the Market )
4. Verify that the selected book opens on the READ page.
5. Verify the 'PDF' icon is enabled under Download section
6. Click the 'PDF' icon.
7. Verify that the book opens in the s3.amazonaws.com page
8. Click on the download button at the top right corner
9. Verify that the 'Save As' dialog opens with Save and Cancel button
10. Click on the 'Save' button
11. Verify that the book downloaded in a PDF format successfully.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close ‘alpha.bloomlibrary.org’ site.
