# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the basic book.
2. Verify it opens in the edit mode.
3. Click the Add page button.
4. Verify Add page dialog opens.
5. Click 'Just a Picture' template.
6. Click the Add page button.
7. Verify the picture page is added.
8. Click the 'Change Image' button.
9. Verify the Image toolbox opens.
10. Enter 'Car' in the Search box.
11. Verify Car image appears in the image box.
12. Click the Car image.
13. Click the OK button.
14. Verify the picture appears on the Picture page.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close Bloom application.
