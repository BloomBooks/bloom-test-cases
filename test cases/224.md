# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the "Other Collection" button from the toolbar.
2. Verify that the "Open/Create Collections" dialog box is displayed.
3. Click the "Create New Collection" button.
4. Verify that the "Create New Bloom Collection" dialog box is displayed.
5. Type "english" in the "Search by name, code, or country" field.
6. Verify "English en" is displayed.
7. Select "English en" in the "Choose Language" dialog box.
8. Verify that the "Next" button is enabled.
9. Click the "Next" button.
10. Verify that the page navigates to the "Font and Script" page.
11. Click the "Next" button.
12. Verify that the page navigates to the "Give Language Location" page.
13. Click the "Next" button.
14. Verify that the page navigates to the "Collection Name" page.
15. Verify there is an error message 'There is already a collection with that name, at C:\Users\UserName\documents\bloom\english book. Please pick a unique name' appears on the dialog box.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close Bloom application.
