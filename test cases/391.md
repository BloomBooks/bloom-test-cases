# Steps

**Setups Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click a Basic book.
2. Verify ‘MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE’ button is available on the right pane.
3. Click the Close button.
4. Verify the Bloom closes.
5. Open a browser window.
6. Enter ‘https://bloomlibrary.org/language:en/book/b9LEDelBFg?lang=en’ in a Search bar.
7. Press the Enter key.
8. Verify the book (eg., Wild Animals)opens on the page.
9. Click the 'Translate into your language' - Download into Bloom Editor' button.
10. Verify Downloading book progress dialog appears.
11. Verify Download complete dialog appears with OK button.
12. Click the OK button.
13. Verify the 'Wild Animals' book opens in the Bloom application.
14. Verify MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button is available for translation.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the Basic book.
2. Close Bloom application.
