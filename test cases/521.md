# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Open Bloom application to a collection with a pro subscription.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the “Basic Book” under the “Templates” area.
2. Verify that the  “Basic Book” template is displayed in the "Right pane".
3. Click the "MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE" button.
4. Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.
5. Click the ‘Add Page’ button at the bottom left of the page.
6. Verify that the “Add Page..” dialog box is displayed.
7. Select the “Just a Picture” page under the “Basic Pages” option.
8. Click the “ADD PAGE” button.
9. Verify that the “Add Page..” dialog box is closed.
10. Verify that the new “Just a Picture” page is displayed.
11. Click the Hamburger menu from the top right corner.
12. Verify that the “Talking Book Tool” menu is displayed.
13. Click the “more” button on the right pane.
14. Click the “Canvas Tool” checkbox.
15. Verify the Canvas Tool menu option displays.
16. Click the Video icon.
17. Drag and drop the Video icon to the picture pane.
18. Verify that the empty video overlay icon is displayed.
19. Click on the empty video overlay icon.
20. Drag and drop the Video overlay icon within the page.
21. Verify that the Video overlay icon is moved to the desired location.
22. Verify that the Video overlay icon is displayed properly.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the Basic book.
2. Close Bloom application.
