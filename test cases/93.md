# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the ‘Get Help’ button (?) in the top-right corner.
2. Verify a pop up menu appears.
3. Click the “Report a Problem” option.
4. Verify that the “Report a Problem" dialog box opens.
5. Click the “LEARN MORE” hyperlink.
6. Verify that the “LEARN MORE” page is opened.
7. Scroll to the end of the page.
8. Verify that there is no “Close” button displayed on that page.
9. Click the “Back” button.
10. Verify that the page navigates to the “Report a Problem" dialog box.
11. Click the “CANCEL” button.
12. Verify that the “Report a Problem" dialog box is closed.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close Bloom application.
