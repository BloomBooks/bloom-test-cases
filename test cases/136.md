# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Download a book “- 5 - On Trial for God” from the given link : https://bloomlibrary.org/EFL-education-for-life-org/EFL-BibleStories-Main/EFL-LLL-Bible-Stories-Print/book/sg4m5HohsD
2. Verify the book opens in the Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the 'MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE' button.
2. Verify the book opens in the edit view.
3. Click the Publish tab.
4. Click the PDF & Print button.
5. Verify Publish to PDF & Print view opens.
6. Click the Booklet Insides mode under Booklet Mode.
7. Verify the PDF book appeared on the middle pane.
8. Scroll down to the 3rd page. (or 4th page).
9. Verify the margins of the left and right page are evenly aligned.
10. Enter “https://bloomlibrary.org/EFL-education-for-life-org/EFL-BibleStories-Main/EFL-LLL-Bible-Stories-Print/book/sg4m5HohsD” in the browser window.
11. Click the READ button.
12. Verify the book opens in the Bloom player.
13. Navigate to the 4th page by clicking the Next button.
14. Verify the margins of the page are evenly aligned.
15. Click the Next button.
16. Verify 5th page opens.
17. Verify the margins of the page are evenly aligned.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete the “- 5 - On Trial for God” book.
2. Close Bloom application.
