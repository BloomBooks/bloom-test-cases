# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application
2. Click the Settings button.
3. Verify the Settings dialog opens.
4. Click the Bloom Subscription tab.
5. Verify the Bloom Subscription tab opens.
6. Enter 'Test-727011-1339' in the Subscription code.
7. Verify the Restart button appears instead of the OK button.
8. Click the Restart button.
9. Verify Bloom gets restarted.

**Action Steps**

1. Click 'The Moon and the Cap' book under Sample Shells.
2. Click MAKE A BOOK USING THIS SOURCE button.
3. Verify the book opens in the edit view.
4. Click the 'A5 Portrait' dropdown list box.
5. Verify the list box opens.
6. Select 'Device 16x9 Portrait' option.
7. Verify the Page layout is changed to Portrait.
8. Click Page 1.
9. Click the Hamburger button.
10. Verify the TBT menu opens.
11. Verify the text is highlighted.
12. Press and hold the 3)Speak button.
13. Record "All of us went to the fun fair"
14. Verify the 4)Check button is enabled.
15. Click the More..option under TBT menu.
16. Verify the More..menu opens.
17. Select "Motion Tool" check box.
18. Click the Preview button.
19. Verify that the Motion feature plays along with the audio.
20. Click Page 2
21. Click Page 1.
22. Verify that no blank paragraph is inserted at the beginning of the text block.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close Bloom application.
