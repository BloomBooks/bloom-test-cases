# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click 'A Family Learn about Immunisations' book thumbnail.
2. Verify there is a triangle button appearing on the thumbnail.
3. Click the triangle button.
4. Verify an option menu appears on the screen.
5. Click Delete a Book option.
6. Verify 'Confirm Delete' dialog message appears with 'Delete' button.
7. Click the Delete button.
8. Verify the book is deleted from the Book Collection pane.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close Bloom application.
