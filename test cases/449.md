# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Download 'Hadiyaa' book from https://issues.bloomlibrary.org/youtrack/issue/BL-3542
2. Launch the Bloom application

**Action Steps**

1. Verify that the “Hadiyya” books were displayed in the collection tab.
2. Select the book “1a Aturee Danto'oo” under the “Hadiyya” area.
3. Verify that the “1a Aturee Danto'oo” preview is displayed in the right pane.
4. Click the “EDIT THIS BOOK” button.
5. Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.
6. Click page 1.
7. Verify that the page loads correctly and without any delay.
8. Click page 5.
9. Verify that the page loads correctly and without any delay.
10. Click the “Collections” tab.
11. Select the book “4b Albachcha” under the “Hadiyya” area.
12. Verify that the “4b Albachcha” preview is displayed in the right pane.
13. Click the “EDIT THIS BOOK” button.
14. Verify it opens in the "Edit" mode.
15. Click page 1.
16. Verify that the page loads correctly and without any delay.
17. Click page 5.
18. Verify that the page loads correctly and without any delay.
19. Click page 10.
20. Verify that the page loads correctly and without any delay.
21. Click page 12.
22. Verify that the page loads correctly and without any delay.
23. Click page 15.
24. Verify that the page loads correctly and without any delay.
25. Click page 18.
26. Verify that the page loads correctly and without any delay.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Delete 'Hadiyaa' book.
2. Close Bloom application.
