# Steps

**Setup Steps**

1. Launch Bloom application.

**Action Steps**

1. Click the Help(?) option.
2. Verify the Help menu opens.
3. Click the 'Report a Problem' option.
4. Verify the Report a Problem dialog opens.
5. Click the Learn More button.
6. Verify the error report appears on the dialog.
7. Press Ctrl+A to select all information.
8. Press Ctrl+C to copy all the information.
9. Double click on the Notepad icon.
10. Verify the Notepad editor opens.
11. Press Ctrl+V to paste it.
12. Verify all the information from the error report appears on the Notepad.

**Cleanup Steps**

1. Close Bloom application.
